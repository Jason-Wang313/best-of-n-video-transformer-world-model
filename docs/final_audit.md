# Final Audit

Status: paper-worthy v1

Rationale: the repository is complete enough for a controlled-position paper draft. It has executable experiments, a small learned video artifact, a finite-law validation, counterfactual video figures, scoped repair gates, and a claim audit.

Main limits:

- The environment is synthetic and intentionally small.
- The learned transformer is smoke-scale.
- Repair evidence is simulator-specific.
- There is no hardware validation or broad benchmark suite.

Blocked scope: broad video-modeling victory claims, robot-planning victory claims, blanket Best-of-N harm claims, blanket criticism of all video predictors, universal action-check repair claims, and physical robotics evidence claims.
